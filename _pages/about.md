Welcome! I am an associate professor with tenure at the [School of Data Science](https://sds.cuhk.edu.cn/), [The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/).

Research Interests
---
Distributed optimization, federated learning, large-scale machine learning, multi-agent networks.

See [here](https://pu-shi.github.io/lab/) for our research topics, and [here](https://pu-shi.github.io/publications/) for our publications.

Openings
---
We are always looking for self-motivated students with solid mathematical background and research interests in distributed optimization, large-scale optimization algorithms, machine learning, federated learning, game theory, etc.
