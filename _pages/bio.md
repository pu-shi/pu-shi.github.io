I am an associate professor with tenure at the [School of Data Science](https://sds.cuhk.edu.cn/), [The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en). From 2019 to 2026, I was an assistant professor at the same university; from 2019 to 2020, I was affiliated with the Institute for Data and Decision Analytics (iDDA), which was later merged into SDS. Prior to joining CUHK-Shenzhen, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Florida from 2016 to 2017, advised by [Alfredo Garcia](https://engineering.tamu.edu/industrial/profiles/garcia-alfredo.html); at Arizona State University from 2017 to 2018, advised by [Angelia Nedić](https://angelia.engineering.asu.edu/); and in the Division of Systems Engineering at Boston University from 2018 to 2019, advised by [Alex Olshevsky](https://sites.bu.edu/aolshevsky/) and [Yannis Paschalidis](https://sites.bu.edu/paschalidis/people/yannis-paschalidis/). 

I received my B.S. in Engineering Mechanics from Peking University in 2012 and my Ph.D. in Systems Engineering from the University of Virginia in 2016, advised by [Alfredo Garcia](https://engineering.tamu.edu/industrial/profiles/garcia-alfredo.html).

My research has been funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data (SRIBD), Shenzhen Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for Society (AIRS), and Shenzhen Science and Technology Program.

I enjoy playing soccer, table tennis, badminton and other fun sports.
